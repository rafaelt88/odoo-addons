What types of combo promotions can I create with this module?

You can create Buy X Get Y, product bundles, and other flexible combo offers to boost sales and customer engagement.

How does the module support upsell and cross-sell strategies?

The module allows you to configure upsell and cross-sell offers on product pages, in the cart, and during checkout to increase average order value.

What is the 'Frequently Bought Together' feature?

This feature suggests related products that are often purchased together, making it easy for customers to add multiple items to their cart with one click.

Is the module fully integrated with the Odoo website?

Yes, all promotion features are available on the Odoo website, including shop, cart, and checkout pages.
